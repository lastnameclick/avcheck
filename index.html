Show-Ready Requirements

  • QLab-compatible format (WAV, AIFF, MP3, M4A, AAC, CAF)
  • Stereo audio (2 channels)
  • Integrated loudness: -24 to -14 LUFS
  • True peak below 0 dBFS (no clipping)
  • Full frequency range (no extreme filtering)
  • Clean recording (not phone/laptop mic)
